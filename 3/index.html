Part 1. Defining Correspondences
To transform one face into another, it's necessary to establish pairs of matching points on both images, maintaining consistent order. I employed ginput to select these points on an image. After obtaining two matching sets of points (one set for each face), we calculate the average of these points and then perform Delaunay triangulation on this average set of key points. In total of 45 points are chosen on each face.
